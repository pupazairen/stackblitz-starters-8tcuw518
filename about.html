What This Is

Azairen Studios is a narrative system built around interconnected universes. Each story operates independently, but all are part of a larger structure.

How It Works

Stories are not isolated. Events, characters, and systems overlap across different formats. A single moment may appear as narrative, investigation, or anomaly depending on where it is accessed.
